# Business model — SSD

Independent public-sector market-entry compliance for South Sudan.

- South Sudan Public Procurement and Disposal of Assets Authority
  (established under the Public Procurement and Disposal of Assets Act,
  2018) public procurement -- no self-service e-procurement portal
  independently found by this iteration; the Ministry of Finance and
  Planning's own domain (mof.gov.ss) failed to resolve this session
  (see `src/marketentry/facts.cljc`)
- Business Registry Certificate of Incorporation, Ministry of Finance
  Tax Identification Number (TIN), and Chamber of Commerce registration
  (per the World Bank Doing Business 2020 South Sudan profile's own
  procedure table, read directly)
- Investment Certificate registration with the (Southern) Sudan
  Investment Authority / Ministry of Investment, per the Investment
  Promotion Act, 2009's own Chapter VI, independently corroborated by
  the live eservices.gov.ss/investment portal
- Public Procurement and Disposal of Assets Act, 2018 Section 4(7)-(8)
  external-beneficiary-procurement local-registration gate -- bars a
  non-compliant filing from proceeding when treaty/agreement-funded
  procurement favouring an external beneficiary has not registered its
  contractor, insurer, AND carriage/transport all in South Sudan
