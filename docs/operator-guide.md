# Operator guide — SSD

Portal: no self-service e-procurement portal was independently found by
this iteration -- the South Sudan Public Procurement and Disposal of
Assets Authority (Public Procurement and Disposal of Assets Act, 2018)
is the national regulator; the Republic of South Sudan's unified
e-Government portal (eservices.gov.ss) hosts a live Business Registry
Service, Revenue Authority, Customs, Trade, and Investment touchpoint.
Entity: Business Registry Certificate of Incorporation + Ministry of
Finance TIN + Chamber of Commerce registration. Externally-favouring
treaty-funded procurement engagements also clear the Act's own Section
4(7)-(8) local-registration gate (contractor, insurer, AND carriage all
registered in South Sudan) before a filing can proceed. Human-gated
filing only.
